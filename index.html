About Us

As a young girl, Annie spent many afternoons at her grandmother's house after school. Her Nana, as she called her, had a finicky, white cat named Beau. When they would sit down to eat dinner, they would frequently find Beau waiting for a plate. Her grandfather would often joke, "What do you think this is? Beau's Bistro?" and Annie's restaurant's name was born.

After being in business for over 15 years, Annie continues to enjoy providing all guests with delicious meals. Whether they are on their way to work or about to begin a night out on the town, Beau's Bistro offers a multitude of options hoping that everyone can find comfort in the food they enjoy here.
